Setting up a **WAVESPAS inflatable spa** is straightforward, but following these steps will ensure optimal performance and longevity.

## Step 1: Choose a Location

Place your spa on a flat, level surface – concrete, deck, or compacted gravel. Avoid grass as it can shift. Ensure access to a GFCI-protected outlet within 10 feet.

## Step 2: Unbox and Inflate

Lay out the spa liner and connect the pump. Turn the dial to “Inflate” – it takes about 3-5 minutes. Once firm, attach the cover.

## Step 3: Fill with Water

Use a garden hose to fill to the marked line (usually 80% full). Do not overfill. Add the included chemical starter pack according to instructions.

## Step 4: Heat and Enjoy

Set the desired temperature (up to 104°F). Heating takes 6-12 hours. While waiting, check our [WAVESPAS review](/posts/2026-05-11-wavespas-review/) for performance tips.

## Maintenance Tips

- **Water care**: Test pH and chlorine levels weekly. Use the floating dispenser.
- **Filter cleaning**: Rinse the cartridge every 3-4 days, replace monthly.
- **Winter use**: The freeze protection system kicks in automatically. Keep the cover on when not in use.
- **Storage**: Drain completely, dry, and store in a cool, dry place.

## Troubleshooting

- **Error codes**: Most are resolved by restarting the pump. Check the manual.
- **Not heating**: Ensure the filter is clean and the cover is on.
- **Leaks**: Patch kits are available; small punctures can be repaired easily.
