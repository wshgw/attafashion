## 1. Bobbi Ankle Boot — The Icon

The Bobbi is Merry People's flagship and best-seller — for good reason. It's a Chelsea-style ankle boot that looks like a fashion boot but performs like a rugged wellington.

**Key features:**
- Chelsea boot silhouette with pull tabs
- 3mm neoprene lining for warmth and comfort
- Built-in arch support with removable insole
- Natural rubber, 100% waterproof
- Available in 15+ colors

**Best for:** Everyday wear — commuting, running errands, dog walks, light outdoor activities. It's the "does everything" boot.

**Colors to consider:** Black (most versatile), Sand (neutral chic), Rust (warm statement), Fuchsia (bold personality).

## 2. Tully Platform Boot — The Statement

The Tully takes the Bobbi's comfort DNA and adds a chunky platform heel and contemporary silhouette. It's Mary People's fashion-forward offering.

**Best for:** Festival-goers, fashion lovers, anyone who wants to make a statement. Not ideal for long hikes or heavy outdoor use.

## 3. Fergie Tall Welly — Full Coverage

When you need full-height protection — think deep puddles, farm chores, heavy rain — the Fergie delivers. It features an adjustable calf strap, neoprene lining, and a rugged traction sole.

**Best for:** Countryside walks, gardening, heavy rain, anyone who prefers the classic tall wellington look.

## 4. Kids' Bobbi — Rainy Day Adventures For Little Ones

Merry People makes a kids' version of the Bobbi with the same natural rubber construction and neoprene lining. Easy on/off pull tabs make them parent-friendly.

**Best for:** Children aged 2-10 who love jumping in puddles.

## Sizing Guide

Merry People boots fit **true to size** with a snug feel. Key tips:

- **Normal feet:** Order your regular shoe size
- **Wide feet:** Size up one full size
- **Between sizes:** Size up
- **Need extra room:** Remove the insole for approximately 0.5 size more space
- **Kids' sizes:** Follow the size chart on their website

> **Pro tip:** The neoprene lining means the boots will feel snug at first but will conform to your foot shape after a few wears.

## Care and Maintenance

To extend the life of your Merry People boots:

1. **Clean** with mild soap and water after muddy use
2. **Dry** naturally at room temperature — never near direct heat
3. **Protect** with silicone spray every 2-3 months to keep natural rubber supple
4. **Store** in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
5. **Remove insoles** to air out after heavy use
